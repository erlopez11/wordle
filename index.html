⭐️ How to Play ⭐️

Welcome to Wordle: the game where you have 6 tries to guess the chosen Wordle Word.

To start, use the keyboard provided to enter your word

A

A grey tile means the letter isn't in the Wordle Word

B

A yellow tile means the letter is in the word but the wrong place

C

A green tile means the letter is in the word an in the correct spot

Remember you must input a 5 letter word in order to submit your answer. But don't worry, you will get an alert if the letter is too short or invalid

Let the game begin! 💫
